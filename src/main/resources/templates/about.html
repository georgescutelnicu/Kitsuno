About Kitsuno

Kitsuno is a personal project I created to help myself master the essentials of the Japanese language —Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji— while expanding my vocabulary in an engaging and accessible way. By building Kitsuno, I aimed to combine my passion for Japanese language learning with the opportunity to grow as a developer and create something truly useful for my own studies.

This website wasn’t built for commercial purposes. Instead, Kitsuno represents a dedication to both Japanese language learning and personal growth as a developer. Combining a love for languages and technology, Kitsuno showcases what’s possible when creativity, learning, and development come together.

Thank you for exploring Kitsuno!
