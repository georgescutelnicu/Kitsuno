FAQ

Is it okay for beginners?

Absolutely! Kitsuno starts from the basics, so no prior knowledge is needed. It’s perfect for anyone new to the japanese language.

What will I learn on this platform?

Kitsuno focuses on three key areas of Japanese learning: kana, kanji, and essential vocabulary. You’ll learn how to read and write both kana systems, understand and recognize kanji, and build a solid foundation of commonly used words.

How does the flashcard system work?

Our flashcard system is centered on helping you master kanji efficiently. You can create your own flashcards by adding your own words or notes to kanji characters to help your learning.

What does Kitsuno API cover?

Kitsuno API provides endpoints for working with Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji characters, as well as managing flashcards to support and personalize your learning experiecne. To access the API, you’ll need to register and obtain an API key from your profile page.

Is there any cost to use the platform?

No, Kitsuno is entirely free to use. All the content, resources, and practice tools are accessible without any subscription fees or hidden costs. The goal is to make learning Japanese fun and easy to start.
